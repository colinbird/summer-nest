What made us smile

  • Volume limits were easy to configure, we found the configuration under the gateway account. Although it wasn't in the typical settings screen.

    • What was confusing, did you encounter any issues?

    • We were not able to configure a volume limit in CAD since our reporting currency is USD.
    • The exercise asks to set D then C then E gateway priority but this is not possible with the weighted gateway selection rule. We effectively achieved this by setting the weights to D:999999999999999 and C:1 (and E:0).

    • The limits are calculated by cron job running every 5 minutes. This is not ideal for a live system. We would prefer to see the limits calculated in real-time. It's possible to go over the volume limits within 5 minutes.
